About Moxwell

Moxwell is reimagining insurance with AI solutions designed for speed, clarity, and trust. We help insurers resolve claims faster while keeping customers informed at every step.

Our AI Claims Assistant streamlines the process by detecting fraud, generating summaries, and drafting communications within existing workflows. Alongside this, our CX AI Transparency provides real-time updates in clear, simple language across chat and voice channels.

Built for mid-size insurers and brokers, Moxwell delivers affordable, plug-and-play tools that integrate seamlessly with modern insurance platforms. Our focus is on practical adoption — no heavy enterprise projects, just solutions that work in today’s cloud-native, mobile-first world.

Our mission is to simplify insurance, empower adjusters, and create meaningful experiences for customers — helping the industry move faster, smarter, and with real impact.
